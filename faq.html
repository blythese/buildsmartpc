FAQ

Q: Is building a PC hard?

A: Not really! It’s like putting together adult LEGO. With the right guidance, anyone can do it.

Q: Do I need special tools?

A: A Phillips head screwdriver is all you need for most builds.

Q: How do I know parts are compatible?

A: Use tools like PCPartPicker to check compatibility before buying.

Q: What if my PC doesn’t turn on?

A: Don’t panic! Recheck all your connections.
